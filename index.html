About me

I help companies turn data into real business value.

ST

I'm a Machine Learning and AI professional, currently Principal AI/ML at Eli Lilly & Company. Over the last nine years I've worked across MNCs and startups — building on a foundation in statistics and classical ML, then deep learning, NLP and LLMs.

My specialty is transforming messy data into actionable insight and shipping models that optimize real processes. I've built and led advanced-analytics work and delivered ML, NLP and deep-learning projects across very different industries.

Right now my focus is agentic AI and multi-modal systems — understanding how autonomous agents will reshape how we work, and building the systems that get us there. Away from data, you'll find me on long walks with my favourite tunes, on a badminton or table-tennis court, or with my family.
